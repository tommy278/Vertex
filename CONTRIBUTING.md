# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in contributing to **Vetrex**!

Even though Vetrex is a fun project, I still take it seriously—so any help is greatly appreciated.

## Ways to Contribute

1. **Break stuff**
   Try to break the compiler and then create an issue describing what happened.
2. **Add features**
   Implement something new and open a pull request.
3. **Improve documentation**
   Help make things clearer and easier to understand.
---

Thanks again for your interest, and see you around :>
